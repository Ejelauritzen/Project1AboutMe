Things I like

Three things I like to do are woodworking, video games, and traveling. I love make things with wood, my dad has a workshop and I have spent countless hours in it making different things. In our home I have made our bed frame, coffee table, dining table, shoe bench, and others. My favorite project was when I made our bar table. With this project I used a large piece of natural pecan wood that is just stunning.
I also like video games. When I was younger I loved the game Zelda Ocarina of Time. I was lost in the amazing world that it presented to me and spent hours playing. Now that I am older I play a wider variety of games. My current favorite is Assassin's Creed Odyssey.
Traveling is something I dream constantly about. I have visited a fair amount of different countries. I have been lucky enough to vacation as much as I have, but I am always planning the next adventure. The current dream is to visit Japan. The plan is to go in August for about ten days.
